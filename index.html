Facilities provided by the Pet House Center

Grooming

Do you want your pets to have a beautiful fur so you can show it off to the world? Come to our grooming center! We only use high-quality professional grooming shampoos and conditioners for our friends’ healthy skins and coats.

Fancy a traditional or modern look for your pet? We regularly educate ourselves and follow the trends of pet grooming to satisfy our customers’ grooming requirements.

All grooming facilities and tools are washed, cleaned, and sterilized daily to avoid the spreading of any contagious diseases or ectoparasites, such as fleas and ticks.

Hotel

When you are going to have a vacation or business trip but worry about your pets living alone inside your house for a few days? Our hotel has the answer! Our comfortable accommodations & safety-certified associates provide a welcoming place for dogs & cats to stay while you’re away!

We offer a safe, clean, temperature-controlled environment, playtime, exercise, healthy meals & more. We also have 2 types of hotel room, which both have their own uniqueness. What are the rooms, you wonder? The exclusive and extravagant service, VIP room and the simple but homey vibe, Normal room.

Breeding Center

Would you like to have a new member for your marvelous pet family? Our experienced team have many years’ experience when it comes to pet births and have delivered more than a couple of babies via emergency cesarean!

Your pets' new babies will need vet health checks and depending on the species, vaccinations, flea & worm treatments, microchipping, training and tailored nutrition for strong growth.

Using our ultrasound imaging machine, we can confirm pregnancy in female dogs and cats from around 15 – 30 days gestation. Our maternity report includes a full set of digital images emailed to you (including video if possible), together with an “Estimated Date of Delivery” report from calculations done by our obstetrics machine. We will also make an estimate of how many puppies or kittens your pet is expecting if possible.

Pick-up & Drop

Does your pet need to go to our center but you are too busy to go outside? We got you covered! We will show up to your house, pick up your pet, take them to the center for our services, and (if needed) pick them up and bring them home and even walk them if you need us to!

All of our services are delivered by a team of trained Pet Care Specialists, all of whom are fully bonded and insured. Each visit is tracked and verified with state-of-the-art GPS technology to let you know exactly when and where your pets are being cared for.

Vet Care

Even when your pets seem healthy and proactive, prevention wellness examination is still needed regularly. In some cases, early treatment can be more effective and affordable. We provide complete laboratory services for all stages of life and provides comfortable overnight and day care facility, with VIP and normal service options

Our team and clinic is always ready to provide treatment for emergency cases, even on Sundays and public holiday. We also provide pet wellness examination at the comfort of your home, see our contact in the About Us Page

Our Pharmacy carries wide range of medical and food supplies for your pets. We provide swimming pool for less stressful rehabilitation and treatment
